About Us

About Us

In today's world, businesses thrive on connections that cross oceans and continents. At CP Freight Forwarders LLP, we are more than a logistics company—we are a global trade enabler. For decades, we've been trusted by exporters, importers, manufacturers, distributors, and corporates to simplify international shipping and ensure their cargo reaches destinations with efficiency, compliance, and care.

With an integrated suite of services—ocean freight, air freight, customs clearance, road and rail transport, project logistics, and supply chain solutions—we deliver end-to-end freight forwarding that combines speed, cost-efficiency, and transparency. Whether it's small parcels, bulk shipments, or complex industrial cargo, our solutions are tailored to your business.

At CP Freight Forwarders LLP, every consignment matters—because we don't just move cargo, we move businesses forward.

Why Us ?

End-to-End Logistics Under One Roof

Global Network, Local Strength
